Display flex is a property of flexbox.
To make a div boxes center use justify-content: center;. And to make them centered vertically use align-items: center;.
justify-content has more properties. Below are some examples.
flex-direction: column; property also exists, as well as column-reverse.

To make flex wrap with many properties at the center, use align-content: center;.
There is also wrap-reverse, flex-flow, and the gap property to give spaces, including row-gap and column-gap.
